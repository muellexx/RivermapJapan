About Page

This homepage is aimed as a help for kayaking in Japan. The map shows river sections that are used by for Kayaking, including the current water level, the difficulty of the river and the start and end points.

This homepage is currently still in the developing process, currently on Version 2.0.
Planned improvements for the future:

  • Add Japanese language
  • Add a page for users to propose river sections
  • Improve the information that is shown about the rivers
  • Greatly extend the database
  • Feature to save favorite rivers and directly show these
  • More points to come ...

Disclaimer: White Water Sports can be associated with considerable risks. White water should therefore only be used by experienced people or under the supervision and guidance of an experienced leader. The data of the river level is automatically generated and the difficulty is only unofficial information, misjudgements are possible!
All information, especially the river information is given without guarantee and does not replace detailed clarifications like river guides, experts, research etc. The situation on site and the subjective perception can deviate greatly from these values. A river can change constantly and suddenly!
Navigating a river or a spot is always the responsibility of the individual and is at your own risk.
Paddling a river when the water level is unsuitable is associated with a considerably greater risk, which can even have legal and insurance related consequences.
Any liability by the operators of www.rivermapjapan.co.jp is rejected.

Creator: This homepage is currently developed by a Swiss guy, who lives and works in Fukushima as an Engineer. The idea for creating the page came from a similar website in Switzerland (www.rivermap.ch).
